The Broken Experience

The "Dust and Polish" Distraction.

Your technicians are in the middle of a delicate gel manicure. The phone rings. They either have to politely excuse themselves (ruining the client's relaxing experience), rush to wash their hands, or let it go to voicemail. If it goes to voicemail, that prospect simply calls the next nail salon down the street. It's a lose-lose situation that restricts your monthly revenue and frustrates your staff.

The Autonomous Agent

The Flawless Front Desk.

Our AI Voice Agent picks up the phone on the first ring. It sounds remarkably human, warmly greeting clients. It can easily answer questions like "How much is a dip powder removal?" or "Do you have time for two pedicures at 3 PM today?" It checks your real-time calendar (like Vagaro, Booker, or Phorest), reserves the slot, and lets your technicians focus entirely on the art of nails without a single interruption.

Designed specifically for Nail Salons

Direct Calendar Sync

Our AI receptionist connects securely to your salon's scheduling software. When a client calls to book an acrylic fill, the AI checks available times, offers options, and locks the appointment in automatically.

Service and Pricing Knowledge

We train your AI receptionist explicitly on your nail salon's menu. Whether it's the difference between Gel-X and SNS, or the price of custom nail art, the AI answers flawlessly.

Group Booking Coordination

Bridal parties and birthday groups are highly lucrative. The AI receptionist can handle multi-person booking requests, ensuring enough technicians are available for concurrent services.
