Beyond simple call routing

Intelligent Native Dispatch Scheduling

The agent hooks directly into your scheduling software. It negotiates arrival windows verbally ("Would a 2 PM to 4 PM window work for you?") and silently creates the dispatch ticket with all homeowner details in the background.

Omni-System CRM Sync

Say goodbye to manually typing up call notes while parked in your truck. Our system transcribes the entire call, pulls out key details like gate codes or specific issues, and pushes the summary directly to your technicians' mobile apps.

Dynamic Objection Handling

Armed with your internal company playbook, the voice agent can handle pricing pushback ("Why is the diagnostic fee $99?"), explain service warranties, or guide users through baseline emergency shutoffs while a truck is en route.
