The Broken Experience

The Scheduling Nightmare.

Hiring human receptionists for a busy hair salon is extremely difficult. They get overwhelmed during rush hours, mistakes are made with color processing times, and double-bookings destroy the client experience. When stylists have to answer their own phones, they abandon their client in the chair—breaking the luxury experience you strive to provide. Meanwhile, missed calls turn into lost business.

The Autonomous Agent

Intelligent Salon Booking.

Deploy a voice agent that perfectly understands the nuances of hair services. If a client asks for "highlights," the AI asks clarifying questions to allocate the correct amount of time. It knows exactly which stylist handles which services. It routes complex emergency color corrections directly to a manager while autonomously handling the 80% of routine bookings, cancellations, and reschedules entirely in the background.

Built for the Flow of Hair Salons

Complex Service Timing

Your AI knows that a partial highlight with a blowout requires specifically separated blocks of time. It accesses your calendar, finds the precise window, and books it seamlessly without causing overlapping conflicts.

Stylist Specific Routing

Clients are fiercely loyal to their stylists. The AI receptionist recognizes return callers, asks if they want to book with "Sarah" again, and finds her exact availability in seconds.

Waitlist Management

When a client cancels via text or phone, the AI can instantly identify the available gap and reach out to the next person on your cancellation waitlist to keep your chairs full at all times.
