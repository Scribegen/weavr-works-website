High-Ticket Field Services (HVAC & Plumbing)

Regional HVAC & Emergency Plumbing Group

Deployed: AI Receptionist & Dispatch Agent

The Bottleneck

The company was spending $7,000/mo on a human answering service for after-hours emergency calls. During peak seasons (freezing winters, heat waves), the answering service would place callers on hold, causing panicked homeowners to hang up and instantly call a competitor. The company estimated they were losing $40,000+ per month in missed emergency high-ticket dispatch jobs.

The AI Solution

We engineered an autonomous AI Receptionist deeply instructed in emergency triage. Connected directly to their ServiceTitan backend, the AI instantly answered infinite concurrent calls 24/7. It dynamically calmed the caller, extracted their address, categorized the severity of the leak/outage, and autonomously dispatched the on-call technician via text.
